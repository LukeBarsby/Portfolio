Final Year Project

For my FYP I explored procedurally generated environments with interactable elements

For this research project, I looked into all areas of Procedural Generation in terrain generation. This allowed me to understand the techniques and principles used when generating landscapes. Areas looked into:

  1. Procedural Generation as a whole and its uses
  2. Pseudo-Random Number Generation
  3. Noise Functions
  4. Continuous Noise Functions
  5. Interpolating Noise/Coherent Noise
  6. 2-Dimensional Noise
  7. Height/Intensity Mapping
  8. Octaves
  9. Mesh Generation
  10. Colour Mapping
  11. Island Generation
  12. Endless Terrain Generation
  13. Interactive Elements
